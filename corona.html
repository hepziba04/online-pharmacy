WHAT IS CORONA/COVID 19

What is Coronovirus and COVID-19 ?

Coronaviruses belongs go a family of virus that infects mainly animals.There are few that may infect humans as well, COVID 19 is one of them. COVID stands for Coronavirus disease, 19 here signifies the year when it first came to knowledge.

Is Coronovirus/COVID-19 contagious/deadly ?

The mortality rate of coronovirus is 3 percent. So this is not amongst the deadliest of viruses. According to WHO most people (about 80%) recover from COVID 19 disease without needing any special treatment. However this COVID 19 is quite contagious.

How does Corona virus spread ?

We humans are the most prevalent carrier of this coronavirus. When an infected person of COVID 19 coughs/sneezes, droplets of fluids like saliva/mucus are spread in the air or nearby objects and surfaces. These tiny droplets can come into direct contact with nearby people who may get infected. Other people may also catch COVID-19 by touching the infected objects or surfaces, then touching their eyes, nose or mouth.

Who is at higher risk and can be killed by this COVID 19 ?

Older people are the ones who are more likely to develop serious illness. Also people with underlying medical problems like hypertension, heart/lung related disease, cancer should take proper precautions.

Medicine/Vaccine for COVID 19

Since this is a new virus, currently there is no sureshot medicine/vaccine available for the treatment of COVID 19.Having said that research is going throughout the world to create a drug/vaccine to treat this disease effectively, however it may take some time.
